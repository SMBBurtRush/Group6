Tools

Microsoft Teams was used as our teams' base for communication. Microsoft Teams was utilised very well by our team. We had biweekly meetings to split up work among team members, checking in on progress and discussing what needs to be done. We also used posts in the group feed to update each other on progress, ask for and offer help when needed, asking for the input of other team members on aspects of the website, and many more things.

To make our group website we used the applications Atom and GitHub Desktop to create, edit, and add all the aspects of our project to the GitHub repository created by team member Shannon with all other team members as collaborators. Each team member added in their own research tasks and other parts were split amongst the members of our group.
