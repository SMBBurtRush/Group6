    All of our team members aspire to work in the I.T. industry in the future. These jobs all require technical I.T. skills such as programming and software testing. Verbal/written communication and teamwork skills are also highly desirable for these roles as they require collaborating with other clients and team members. Time management is also an essential skill to have for all the ideal jobs require working on projects that often have deadlines.

    In order to achieve a pathway into their ideal jobs, Matthew, Marah and Jordan are intending to pursue and Bachelor in I.T. to further develop their skills and provide them with the qualifications to be hired into junior/entry level roles. Both Shannon and Anna plan to utilise online resource to continue to develop their skills in web languages as well as putting their skills in action by working on their own projects.
